LaneSplitter

LaneSplitter is a mobile racing-style game where the player dodges through lanes filled with obstacles at increasing speed. I built this using Unity, focusing on smooth lane-based movement, obstacle generation, and mobile-friendly controls.

2D Survival Shooter

This Crimsonland-style game is a top-down survival shooter. The player uses the mouse to aim and shoot in a procedurally hostile environment. Features include:

  • Mouse-based aiming and movement
  • Enemy AI that chases you when close
  • Three weapons: Pistol, SMG, Laser Gun
  • Health and shield pickups
  • Sound effects and background music
  • Score tracker with leaderboard
